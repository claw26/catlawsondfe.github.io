Guidance

Teacher training: physical health and fitness

Practical training materials for primary and secondary schools to use to train staff to teach about physical health and fitness.

From:
Department for Education
Applies to:
England

Documents

Physical health and fitness training module

MS Powerpoint Presentation, 245KB

Details

This training module supports the physical health and mental wellbeing section of the statutory relationships, sex and health education curriculum.

The slides contain:

  • key knowledge and facts to help teach the statutory guidance
  • activities, templates and examples of good practice to help run a training session
  • guidance to help teachers tackle difficult questions

These slides should be adapted to suit individual school context. This can include adding your own videos and resources to make your teaching training sessions more interactive.

Related topics

When planning your curriculum, consider how you will link these topics.

Physical health and fitness is related to:

  • the national curriculum for PE
  • mental wellbeing
  • healthy eating
  • health and prevention
  • changing adolescent body

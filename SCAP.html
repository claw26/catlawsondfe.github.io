How local authorities should collect and submit data for the annual school capacity survey (SCAP).

The annual school capacity survey (SCAP) collects information on:

  • primary and secondary school capacity for the current academic year
  • primary pupil number forecasts for the next 5 academic years
  • secondary pupil number forecasts for the next 7 academic years
  • plans for changes to the number of primary and secondary places for the next 3 academic years

SCAP is a statutory collection. You must complete statutory collections by law unless there’s a good reason not to. We publish the data you provide in the school capacity statistical releases.

The collection opens on Monday 6 June 2022.

You must submit your census return by Friday 29 July 2022.
